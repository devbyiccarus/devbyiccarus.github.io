Ableton Live Utility Racks 1.0

This is a collection of 15 Audio Effect Racks I made over these 10+ years in music production to speed up my workflow, streamline and leverage the power of Live’s stock devices.

Clipper

This rack harness the power of Live’s stock clippers and simplify the process to just lower the Clip/Ceiling and select the type of clipping (Soft/Hard)

Use the readout value to monitor where the ceiling/output is sitting.

Transition FX - Simple / Advanced

These racks add filtering and spacial effects gradually with just one knob to create tension, similar to plugins like Endless Smile and Transit.

Simple: Just a clean filter and a basic reverb space.

Advanced: More elaborate chain of delays and reverbs with more resonant filters.

Low Pass Filter / High Pass Filter

Pre-mapped and range-limited filters for better automation control.

Use the Devices toggle to collapse the expanded view.

Filters

Basically the Low Pass/High Pass Filter racks in one with a compact view for faster workflow and flexible filtering.

Fader - Pan

Simple Fader and Pan (additional Width and Mute) controls for better automation without affecting the mixer controls.

Split Pan

Control the panning for each channel (L / R) separately.

Dry-Wet Knob

Add a dry/wet knob to any effect or chain of effects.

Just drop everything you want in the “Wet” chain and adjust the Dry/Wet parameter to blend the signals.

Parallel Chain

Easy-to-use parallel splitter to the same channel.

Drop the effect in the “Parallel” chain and mix it using the chain volume within the rack.
Use the mute and solo functions in the rack mixer to turn off or monitor your parallel or dry chain.

Mid-Side Splitter

Simple mid/side splitter for flexible stereo processing and control.

Drop the effects in the indicated position (after the splitter)

Frequency Splitter (2-band & 3-band)

Simple frequency splitter (2 and 3-band options).

Use the Macro Controls toggle to reveal the crossover adjustments.
Drop the effects in the indicated position (after the splitters)

Haas Effect

Simple and streamlined haas effect

Use the Pan Adjust parameter to compensate panning problems created by the effect
Use the Time parameter to adjust the delay time and the L/R parameter to change which channel is delayed

Basic Stereo Imager

Simple stereo imager that uses a similar technique found on Ozone Imager plugin by Izotope.

Use the Stereoize parameter to adjust the stereo delay time
